Message from the Principal

Dear Parents and Students,

Welcome to V K Memorial Public School. Education is not just about academic excellence; it is about character, integrity, and lifelong learning.

Our mission is to provide a holistic environment where students can thrive intellectually and socially. We believe in fostering innovation and excellence in every child.

At V K Memorial, we are committed to shaping the futures of our students by empowering their minds. We invite you to join our community of learners dedicated to integrity.

Warm Regards,

MR. PRINCE PAL SINGH
Principal, V K Memorial Public School
